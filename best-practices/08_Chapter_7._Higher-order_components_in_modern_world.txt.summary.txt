Modernization Directives:
1. Do not introduce HOCs as a primary retrofit strategy for this library; prefer composition, children, `inputComponent`, and targeted render callbacks.
2. If legacy HOCs already exist, keep them pure and isolated while planning a composition-based replacement.
3. Move lifecycle behavior into focused hooks or leaf components instead of wrapping broad subtrees.
4. Use adapter components only at compatibility boundaries where a class-friendly or third-party surface is unavoidable.
5. Measure before memoizing adapter components; only add `memo` when parent-driven re-renders are a demonstrated bottleneck.
